# xthread Demo Video Script

**Target length:** 60-90 seconds  
**Style:** Screen recording with voiceover (or text captions)  
**Mode:** LIGHT MODE (better for social media)

---

## Scene 1: Hook (0-5s)

**Visual:** Side-by-side comparison
- LEFT: Generic AI output ("I think AI is interesting. What do you think?")
- RIGHT: xthread output (Spencer's actual voice style)

**Text overlay:** "Your voice. 10x the reach."

---

## Scene 2: The Problem (5-15s)

**Visual:** Quick montage of:
1. Someone staring at blank tweet composer
2. ChatGPT output that sounds robotic
3. Analytics showing low engagement

**Voiceover/Caption:** 
"You know what you want to say. But writing it, scheduling it, knowing what actually works? That's a whole job."

## Scene 3: Voice Training (15-35s)

**Visual:** Show the voice training flow:
1. Click "Train Your Voice" 
2. Shows X connection pulling real tweets
3. Voice profile being created with:
   - Tone sliders
   - Sample vocabulary
   - Style description

**Voiceover/Caption:**
"xthread learns YOUR voice from your best posts. Not generic AI — you, but better."

**Show:** Profile card with "Voice Match: 94%"

## Scene 4: Content Generation (35-50s)

**Visual:** Creator Hub / Generate page

2. Enter topic: "Just shipped a major feature"

5. Pick one, edit slightly
6. See engagement score: 82/100 with tips

**Voiceover/Caption:**
"Pick a format. Enter your topic. Get content that sounds like you wrote it — with algorithm tips to maximize reach."

## Production Notes

1. **Screen recording tips:**
   - Use light mode (better contrast)
   - Clear browser with no distracting tabs
   - Full-screen the app
   - Use a fake account with demo content if needed

2. **Audio options:**
   - Voiceover (Spencer's voice)
   - Music + text captions only
   - AI voiceover (ElevenLabs)

3. **Key moments to capture:**
   - The "magic moment" when AI generates in user's voice
   - Engagement score improving with edits
   - Calendar view showing consistent schedule

4. **Recommended tools:**
   - Screen recording: OBS, Loom, or ScreenFlow
   - Editing: CapCut (free), DaVinci Resolve, or Premiere
   - Add zoom effects on key UI elements
