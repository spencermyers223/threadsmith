## üìù Usage Notes

### Hook Writing Tips
- Spend 80% of your effort on the first line
- Use numbers when possible ("7 things" not "several things")
- Create curiosity gaps ("Here's why..." makes people click)
- Be specific ("$500M" not "a lot of money")

### Timing Recommendations
- **Alpha Drops:** Morning (8-10am EST) when people research
- **Hot Takes:** Evening (6-9pm EST) for maximum engagement
- **Build in Public:** End of week (Friday) for reflection vibes
- **Market Analysis:** Market hours or right after big moves

### Post Type Selection Guide
| Your Goal | Best Templates |
|-----------|---------------|
| Build credibility | Alpha Drops, Market Analysis |
| Get engagement | Hot Takes, Engagement Hooks |
| Build community | Build in Public, Engagement Hooks |
| Go viral | Hot Takes, Bold Predictions |
| Get saves | Alpha Lists, Data Posts |
