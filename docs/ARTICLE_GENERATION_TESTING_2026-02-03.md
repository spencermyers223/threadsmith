### Evaluation Criteria
1. **Length Consistency**
   - Verify article exceeds 800 words
   - Check for meaningful content, not padding

2. **Structural Integrity**
   - Introduction sets context
   - Body has clear, logical sections
   - Conclusion summarizes key points

3. **Content Quality**
   - Factual accuracy
   - Engaging writing style
   - Appropriate depth for topic

4. **Formatting**
   - Proper use of paragraphs
   - Potential subheadings
   - Coherent transitions
