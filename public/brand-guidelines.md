# ThreadSmith Brand Guidelines

## Brand Overview

ThreadSmith is a content crafting tool that helps researchers and thought leaders transform their ideas into engaging social media content while preserving their authentic voice.

**Brand Essence:** Craftsmanship meets authenticity
**Tagline:** "Your ideas, expertly threaded"

---

## Logo

### Primary Logo
The ThreadSmith logo combines a thread spool icon with the wordmark. The spool represents the crafting of content, while the trailing thread symbolizes the continuous flow of ideas.

### Logo Variations
- **Primary (Full Color):** For dark backgrounds - gold accent with silver/white elements
- **White:** Single color for dark backgrounds
- **Dark:** Single color for light backgrounds
- **Icon Only:** For app icons, favicons, and small applications

### Clear Space
Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "T" in ThreadSmith on all sides.

### Minimum Size
- Full logo: 120px wide minimum
- Icon only: 32px minimum

### Don'ts
- Don't stretch or distort the logo
- Don't change the logo colors
- Don't add effects (shadows, glows, etc.)
- Don't place on busy backgrounds without sufficient contrast

## Typography

### Primary Font
**Inter** - Used for UI elements, body text
- Available from Google Fonts
- Fallback: system-ui, -apple-system, 'Segoe UI', sans-serif

### Font Weights
- **Regular (400):** Body text, descriptions
- **Medium (500):** Buttons, labels
- **Semibold (600):** Headings, emphasis
- **Bold (700):** Large headings, hero text

| Name | Size | Weight | Line Height | Usage |

## Iconography

Use Lucide icons throughout the application for consistency.
- Stroke width: 1.5px (default)
- Size: 20px for inline, 24px for standalone
- Color: Match surrounding text or use Gold for emphasis

## Voice & Tone

### Brand Voice
- **Confident:** We know our craft
- **Helpful:** We're here to elevate your voice, not replace it
- **Professional:** Polished but not stuffy
- **Direct:** Clear communication, no jargon

### Writing Guidelines
- Use active voice
- Keep sentences concise
- Speak to the user directly ("you", "your")
- Avoid technical jargon unless necessary
- Be encouraging, never condescending

### Example Copy
✅ "Your ideas, expertly threaded"
✅ "Turn your research into engaging content"
✅ "Craft posts that sound like you"

❌ "AI-powered content generation platform"
❌ "Leverage our algorithm to optimize engagement"
❌ "Revolutionary content creation solution"
